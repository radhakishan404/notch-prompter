What this gives you

Confident delivery, less cognitive load.

  • Keep your key points visible without memorizing scripts.
  • Stay focused during investor pitches, sales demos, and tutorials.
  • Use one app that feels native and does not depend on cloud services.

How it works

  1. Paste your script into the editor window.
  2. Tune speed, width, height, and opacity.
  3. Play from your menu bar while presenting.

Installation

Install in 4 steps

  1. Download the latest .dmg using the button above.
  2. Open the DMG and drag NotchPrompter.app into Applications.
  3. Launch the app from Applications.
  4. Grant Accessibility permission when prompted to enable global shortcuts.

macOS 13+ required.
